# Cross-Story Patterns — White Mirror Puzzle

## Visual Patterns
- **Circuit board motif**: Appears on covers (IMG_7243, 7244), TOC (IMG_7288), and throughout
- **Geometric logo**: Triangle with arrows (Penrose/impossible triangle variant) — central on some pages
- **Arrow triangle**: Recursive triangle with inward-pointing arrows
- **White mirror object**: Physical mirror appears as prop in multiple stories (RETURNE, System Failure, Dreamscape, etc.)

## Textual / Linguistic Patterns
- **Repeating page numbers**: Page 10, 107, 123 appear across multiple stories (core "mirror" mechanic)
- **Chapter structure**: "Chapter 3" (The White Mirror), "Chapter 5" (The Unseen World / The Day of the Sun) appear in story-03
- **All-caps proclamations**: REVELATIONS OF THE REALM, DREAM OF THE LUDDESSE
- **Epigraph style**: "THE WORLD IS A LIE. THE TRUTH IS OUT THERE. YOU ARE NOT ALONE."

## Structural Patterns
- **12 distinct story sections** (not 5 or 6 as TOC suggests)
- **TOC (IMG_7288) lists 6 titles** that don't match actual story titles:
  1. The Circuit Board → Front Matter / motif
  2. Geometric Logo → RETURNE (story-02)
  3. Arrow Triangle → Deserts/Purple Planet (story-03)
  4. Infinite Books → DEATH OF DESCRIPTION / WHAT DREAMS (story-04/05?)
  5. Tinkered Thinking → DREAM OF THE LUDDESSE (story-11)
  6. White Mirror → The White Mirror chapters (story-03) + System Failure?

## Symbolic / Cryptographic Patterns
- **QR Codes** (IMG_7502): Back matter, likely encode solution data
- **Repeating titles**: "White Mirror" appears as book title, story title, chapter title, object
- **Chapter 3 / Chapter 5 duplication**: Same chapter numbers, different titles across stories
- **Page number mirroring**: Page 10 in RETURNE (starts at p10), page 107 in White Mirror Ch3, page 123 in White Mirror Ch5

## Meta Patterns
- **Book-as-mirror**: The anthology structure mirrors itself — same page numbers, same chapter numbers, same titles across different stories
- **Anthology within anthology**: IMG_7288 (TOC) lists 6 stories; actual book has 12 sections
- **Publisher fiction**: "INFINITE BOOKS" and "TINKERED THINKING" appear as real imprints but are fictional
- **Self-reference**: Stories reference "the book you're holding" (IMG_7246, 7257, 7317, 7353, 7390, 7425, 7460, 7473, 7486, 7499)
